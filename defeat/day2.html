between AAA and Indie Games:

AAA games are big-budget titles developed by large studios, often featuring cutting-edge graphics, massive worlds, and heavy marketing (like GTA VI or Assassin’s Creed Shadows). Indie games, on the other hand, are usually created by smaller teams or individuals with limited budgets, focusing more on creativity, unique art styles, and experimental gameplay (like Hollow Knight: Silksong or The Plucky Squire). In short, AAA prioritizes scale and polish, while indie emphasizes innovation and originality
